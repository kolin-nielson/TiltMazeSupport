About TiltMaze

TiltMaze is an engaging puzzle game where you navigate through increasingly challenging mazes by tilting your device. Use your device's motion controls to guide the ball through the maze, collect coins, and reach the exit.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play the game?

Simply tilt your device to move the ball through the maze. The goal is to reach the exit while avoiding obstacles and collecting coins.

Is TiltMaze free to play?

Yes! TiltMaze is free to play with optional ad-supported features. You can watch ads to continue your game after losing.

The motion controls aren't working properly. What should I do?

Make sure motion sensors are enabled for TiltMaze in your device settings. If issues persist, try restarting the app or your device.

How do I report a bug?

Please use the contact information below to report any bugs or issues you encounter while playing.
